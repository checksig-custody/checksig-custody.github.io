Our Custody Solution

CheckSig provides transparent Bitcoin custody solutions for institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals. Ensuring security, regulatory compliance, smooth inheritance, and privacy, we enjoy industry’s leading insurance policy. CheckSig rejects the “security-by-obscurity” paradigm in favor of a transparent process. Based on a peer reviewed open standard protocol, our segregated custody provides periodic proof-of-reserve and is audited by external firms. Internet disconnected as defense from remote hacks, our solution has two levels. The CheckSig cold storage is always empty except during withdrawals; the “frozen” storage with all assets is managed by multiple external legal entities whose only technical ability is to jointly move funds to cold storage when enabled by us.
We customize our custody services according to client's requirements: long term safe storage, inheritance plan, or arbitrage trading
