CheckSig

Open Transparent Multi-Party Bitcoin Custody Solution for Institutional and High-Net-Worth Individuals

Bitcoin is about _financial sovereignty_ and having direct control of your keys because, as people say, _not your keys, not your bitcoins_. Nonetheless, there are cases like financial institutions, high-net-worth individuals, lack of proper technical skills, etc. where a custodial solution might be convenient or even prescribed. CheckSig implements an open transparent multi-party custody protocol that: * avoids reliance on "security by obscurity", being an auditable *public standard* subject to peer review; * provides transparency to the custodian's clients, so that they can be certain that their assets are held in a secure manner. The guiding principles defining this protocol are: * to store bitcoin under custody in such a way as to minimize risk of loss of funds through theft, error, or other mishap; * to rely on the Bitcoin Core protocol implementation for security wherever possible, rather than inventing new functionality or procedures; * to remain neutral regarding future changes to the Bitcoin protocol.
