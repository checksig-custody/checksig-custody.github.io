Transparent Bitcoin Custody

CheckSig provides transparent bitcoin custody solutions for institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals.
We reject security by obscurity in favor of an extremely auditable process: our main solution is based on a public open standard protocol: multi-level, multi-party, and multi-location, it provides periodic proof-of-reserve blockchain transactions, is hardened by customized multi-signature hardware, and is covered by insurance guarantees.
We customize our custody services according to client's requirements: long term safe storage, inheritance plan, or arbitrage trading, we empower the way you store your bitcoins.
